Doctor Registration Instructions

Welcome to HealthStack - A Smart Healthcare Management System.
Please read the following instructions carefully before registering as a doctor:

  • Fill all your details accurately. Fields marked with * are compulsory.
  • Make sure your email and mobile number are correct and active.
  • Mention your valid specialization and affiliated hospital.
  • After submitting the registration form, your profile will go for admin verification.
  • Please wait patiently for verification by the HealthStack admin team. This process can take up to 24 hours.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once your registration is approved and verified.

Note: Submitting false or incomplete data may lead to rejection.
