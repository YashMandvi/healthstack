HealthStack

Instructions For The User

Smart Healthcare Management System (HealthStack) The HealthStack platform is designed to streamline healthcare management by connecting patients, doctors, hospitals, and insurance providers under a single system. This platform enables efficient management of patient records, doctor consultations, insurance claims, and hospital administration with the vision of 'Seamless Healthcare for All'.

Note:

  • Users should carefully read and understand the above details before registering on the platform.
  • Once a user type (Doctor, Patient, or Hospital) is selected, it cannot be changed later.
  • Registration does not guarantee access to all services unless eligibility is verified by the respective healthcare authorities.

HealthStack aims to simplify healthcare management and ensure efficient, secure, and accessible medical services for everyone.
